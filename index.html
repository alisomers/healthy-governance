Future-Proof Healthcare AI Governance

An Adaptive Framework for Safe, Ethical Innovation

The Problem

Healthcare AI systems often outpace regulatory safeguards, leaving gaps in patient safety, accountability, and equity protections.

The Need

Healthcare demands lifecycle-aligned, risk-adaptive governance frameworks to manage evolving AI systems responsibly.

The Opportunity

Canada can lead globally by establishing a dynamic, patient-centered, future-proof AI governance model that others can follow.
