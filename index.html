Transforming healthcare through adaptive AI governance frameworks

Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming healthcare, offering new capabilities in diagnosis, treatment planning, and system optimization. However, healthcare AI poses unique governance challenges that demand adaptive oversight.

The Problem

Current healthcare regulations, including PHIPA and PIPEDA, were not designed for self-evolving AI models. Key gaps exist in post-deployment monitoring, dynamic patient consent, distributed liability, and cybersecurity resiliency.
